Job Openings

Post-doctoral Position:

We are seeking qualified post-graduates to join the lab as postdocs.

Essential Skills:

Candidates must have a PhD degree in recently five years with a strong record of publication. Must be able to work in a team and have good organization skills.

Ideal Skills:

Experience working with budding yeast system will be an advantage. Also practical knowledge of chromatin biology, histone modifications and epigenetics would be important strength if interested in working on chromatin biology. For those who are seeking to work on synthetic biology, experience in system, chemical or computational biology will be beneficial.

Additional information:

There is no requirement for speaking/reading in Chinese.

To apply for this position, please send your resume and references to jbdai@biomed.tsinghua.edu.cn
